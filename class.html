What are Classes?

Classes are archetypes defining a character's skills, abilities, and role, with 13 official 5th Edition classes. I've included my favorite classes for beginner players, that have easy game mechanics and have potential for interesting backstories

Barbarians

Barbarians are fierce melee combatants using rage.

Fighters

Fighters are masters of weapons and tactics.

Rogues

Rogues are stealthy experts in traps, skills, and precision strikes.

Sorcerers

Sorcerers are innate, charismatic spellcasters.

Warlock

A Warlock's power is drawn from otherworldly patrons.

Wizard

Wizards are studious, powerful arcane spell casters.
